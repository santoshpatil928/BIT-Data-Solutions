Please see below Open Questions and Answers

  • What is EDI?

    Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) is a standardized electronic communication method used to exchange business documents and data between organizations. It enables the transfer of structured information from one computer system to another in a format that both systems can understand.

  • Benefits of Electronic Interchange?

    Improved Efficiency,Reduced Errors,Faster Transaction Processing,Cost Savings,Enhanced Accuracy,Better Business Relationships.

  • What are the various industries using EDI for their Buisnesses?

    Retail,Healthcare,Automotive,Logistics and Transportation,Manufacturing,Finance and Banking,Pharmaceuticals,Technology and Electronics etc..
